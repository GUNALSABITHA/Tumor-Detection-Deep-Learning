Brain Tumor Information

Introduction to Brain Tumors:

  • Brain tumors are abnormal growths of cells in the brain.
  • They can be benign (non-cancerous) or malignant (cancerous).

Types of Brain Tumors:

  • Gliomas
  • Meningiomas
  • Pituitary tumors

Symptoms:

  • Headaches
  • Seizures
  • Cognitive changes

Risk Factors:

  • Family history
  • Exposure to radiation

Diagnosis and Treatment:

  • Imaging tests like MRI and CT scans
  • Surgery
  • Radiation therapy
  • Chemotherapy

Health Advice:

  • Maintain a healthy lifestyle with regular exercise and a balanced diet.
  • Wear helmets during activities at risk of head injury.
  • Regular medical check-ups and early detection are crucial.
